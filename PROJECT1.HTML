SAGAR INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY

Best Engineering College in Bhopal - SISTec

Sagar Institute of Science and Technology (SISTec) - a Sagar Group of Institutions (SGIs) Enterprise, has emerged as one of the Best Engineering Colleges in Bhopal with its state-of-art engineering education. The brand SISTec with its three institutes in Bhopal has created a niche for its innovative implementation of curriculum and is leading amongst the Top Engineering Colleges in MP. SISTec in the coming years aims at bringing revolutionary changes in the field of technical education, so that it embraces itself as one of the best engineering colleges in India.

SGIs aim to encourage innovation and entrepreneurship in specified domain areas. It plans to organise itself as a conglomerate of R&D facility, some of which would be in partnership with different companies and global organisations. These facilities, with latest labs and infrastructure, will be the hub of activity, with active contribution from faculties and students - BE & M.Tech and assert SGIs take as one of the Top Engineering Colleges in MP.

SIPTec & SISTec School of Management Studies

SGIs are also contributing in the field of pharmacy and management education through an effective course execution strategy in its two campuses in Bhopal. These courses are delegated with an aim of creating skilled management and pharmacy human resource.

Besides education the Sagar Group has its remarkable presence in the field of real estate, construction, production and manufacturing.
